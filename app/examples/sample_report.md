# Q3 Operations Review — Lagos Branch

**Date:** 12 September 2025
**Attendees:** Ada (Marketing), Kofi (Sales), Ngozi (Finance), Tunde (Operations)

## Summary

Q3 revenue reached 18,400,000 NGN, up 12% quarter-over-quarter. Growth was driven
by the new wholesale accounts in Ibadan. Margins tightened slightly due to higher
diesel costs for the generators during grid outages.

## Key Decisions

- Marketing will finalize the Q4 campaign by 10 October (owner: Ada).
- Finance will confirm the new wholesale pricing tier by 5 October (owner: Ngozi).
- Operations will pilot a solar-backup system at the Lagos branch in November to
  cut diesel spend (owner: Tunde).

## Risks

  Q4 launch timeline.
- Foreign-exchange volatility could raise the cost of imported components.
